Navigating Your Case

Commonly Asked Questions about Our Process

How do I prepare for my initial consultation?

expand_more

For your first meeting at 2910 Devine Street, please bring all relevant legal documents, court filings, correspondence, and any physical evidence. A written timeline of events is also highly beneficial to help our attorneys quickly grasp the nuances of your case.

What defines your firm's handling of complex cases?

expand_more

Complexity requires a multidisciplinary approach. We assign a lead counsel and a supporting strategy team to every high-stakes matter, ensuring that no detail is overlooked and multiple strategic angles are simultaneously explored.

Do you handle cases outside of Columbia, SC?

expand_more

Yes. While our primary office is in Columbia, we represent clients throughout South Carolina in both state and federal courts. Our appellate practice also handles matters in the South Carolina Court of Appeals and Supreme Court.
